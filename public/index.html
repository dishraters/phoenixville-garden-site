Why back row?

Tomatoes, peppers, and eggplant want full sun and stay upright. Keeping them north/back prevents shade over herbs and flowers.

Why squash middle/edge?

Zucchini is wide and spaghetti squash vines. Give them visible footprints so they do not silently take over.

Why flowers front?

Marigolds and zinnias create a pollinator border, improve the look, and stay low enough for the front.

Why paths?

The paths are for watering, pruning, tying tomatoes, redirecting vines, and harvesting without stepping on soil.
